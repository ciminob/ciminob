About The Program

Oregon State Women’s Lacrosse is a competitive intercollegiate team in the Northwest Women’s Lacrosse Association. We are a student-run organization for women of all playing levels.

  • Expectations

    Active members are enrolled at Oregon State University; attend meetings/practices, have a US Lacrosse membership, and pay dues.

  • Season

    The Oregon State Women's Lacrosse team actively plays games in the Fall, Winter, and Spring seasons.

  • Affiliations

    We are affiliated with the Northwest Women's Lacrosse League (Regional) US Lacrosse (National).

  • Dues

    $850.00 For First Year Players Membership. $750.00 Annual Returning Players Membership.
